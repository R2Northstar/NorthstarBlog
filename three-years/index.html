    Frontier News Network: Happy third birthday Northstar!

    January 27, 2025 6 minute read

    Yep, first official release was in December 2021, three years ago!

    During 2024, progress has been made on:

    • several features, such as mod auto-downloading;
    • a lot of bug fixes were integrated;
    • refactoring the code base to clean it.

    A massive and sincere thank you to all contributors and players.

    Parkour

    This year again, we organized a Parkour tournament, where players could compete on different maps to go through all checkpoints in the shortest amount of time.

    For this occasion, the mod was updated with several features, including map voting and fine-grained route configuration, plus a lot of bug fixes. Full changelog is available here.

    To celebrate Northstar’s birthday, we were lucky enough to have two different trailers, one from Smurfson and another from P3NG00N, check them out:
